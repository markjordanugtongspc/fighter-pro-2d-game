# Fighter Pro 2D: Enhanced Edition

**Fighter Pro 2D** is an adrenaline-pumping, fast-paced action combat game built for the web. Step into the shoes of a cybernetic warrior and battle through challenging AI in **Campaign Mode**, test your endurance in the **Farm Arena**, or challenge your friends in high-stakes **Multiplayer PvP**.

With a deep progression system, centralized stat synchronization, and advanced enemy AI that adapts to your playstyle, every fight is a test of skill and timing. Upgrade your "Vitality Capacity," sharpen your damage, and master the art of the 8+ hit combo to become the ultimate fighter.
